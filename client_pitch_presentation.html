Visual Rebranding Synthesis (Legacy vs. Proposed)

Applying the Xerox rebranding model, the new visual identity transitions from a cold, rigid industrial equipment merchant to an approachable, human-centered technology partner. We lower corporate friction through clean geometry, lowercase typography, and glowing HSL color tokens.

Legacy Identity (Current)
Key Visual Friction Points:
  • Sharp, rigid all-caps Serif typeface (creates emotional distance).
  • Literal CMYK offset-printing grid (limits modern digital-tech scope).
  • Traditional corporate look, difficult to scale on mobile screens.
Proposed B2B Rebrand
Key Strategic Growth Benefits:
  • Friendly lowercase rounded geometric sans-serif (Outfit).
  • Overlapping curves representing roll-to-roll flow & intelligence.
  • High-contrast Deep Crimson & Digital Coral HSL color palette.

Core Color Tokens

Deep Crimson
HSL(354, 75%, 45%)
Digital Coral
HSL(12, 92%, 60%)
Charcoal Slate
HSL(220, 13%, 18%)
