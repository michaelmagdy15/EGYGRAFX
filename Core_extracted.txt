🖋️ Xerox Logo Typography
The Xerox logo uses a custom sans-serif typography rather than a standard off-the-shelf font.

🔍 Characteristics of Xerox Typography
✅ Style
Modern 
Rounded geometric sans-serif 
Clean corporate technology look 

✨ Key Features
• Rounded edges
The letters have soft curves giving:
approachable feel 
innovation / tech personality 

• Lowercase typography
“xerox” is written in lowercase to feel:
modern 
less corporate-rigid 
accessible 

• Bold weight
Creates:
strong visibility 
easy readability in print and signage 

🎨 Evolution of the Xerox Logo
🔴 Old Xerox Logos
Earlier versions used:
heavier corporate typography 
more traditional tech branding 

🔴 Current Logo (2008–present)



Designed by:
Interbrand 
The redesign introduced:
spherical red symbol 
softer typography 
digital-era branding direction

🧠 Branding Insight
The Xerox typography was designed to communicate:
          technology             simplicity             human-centered innovation
👉 The rounded forms reduce the “cold corporate” feeling common in tech brands.
